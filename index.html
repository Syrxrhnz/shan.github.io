Anteikü Café

Our story begins in 2023 along the cobblestone streets of Tanza Place Market. It was here where Anteikü Café opened its first store, offering fresh roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the classic tale, “Tokyo Ghoul,” evoking the tradition of the anime coffee shop.
